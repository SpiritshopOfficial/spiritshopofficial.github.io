Bio

The seasoned party igniter, crafts sets that seamlessly blend deep house, UKGZ, and Amapiano

O'Step, a Brno based DJ deeply steeped in the essence of house dance culture. With over 16 years of music collecting prowess, setting dance floors ablaze in clubs, serenading sandy beach shores, or orchestrating grooves beneath the dazzling starry canopy of a park.

Music

O'Step's music isn't just heard; it's felt

With a groove that moves the soul, he's a maestro of deep house, UK garage, and amapiano. But he's got no problem to serve you hip-hop and soul.

What sets him apart is his inventive spirit, constantly pushing musical boundaries, crafting unique techniques for mixing, and navigating between genres and BPMs with ease.
